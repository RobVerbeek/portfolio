CompeteMore

CompeteMore is a PHP based website which aims to give the students of Thomas More an easy-to-use platform for hosting and participating in competitions. To create this website I teamed up with fellow students to talk to a client. Analyze their needs and design prototypes. This project given during the Requirements Analysis course at Thomas More.

Design Phase

The first step to creating an application is making sure the development team understands the client's needs. That's why in this stage we had a few meetings with the client where we had the opportunity to ask any questions my team members or myself might have had.

Ensuring that the development team is on the same page as the client is key to avoiding problems during development. After the interviews I got to work. I took charge of creating the use-case diagram. This showcases all the functionalities that make up the website and which users are involved.
